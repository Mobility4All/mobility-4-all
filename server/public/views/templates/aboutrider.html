About Mobility 4 All

Mobility 4 All (MO) is a high quality, cost effective, and on-demand transportation for people with disabilities. In it's current state, MO is in pre-revenue stage of its lifetime and is currently seeking investors to get involved with the project. MO currently has a team of 8 members, motivated by a business opportunity and personal connections to transportation for people with disabilites.
This app was built using the SEAN stack, (Postgresql, Express, AngularJS, Node.js).
